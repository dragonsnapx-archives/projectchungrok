What is Chungrok?

Project Chungrok is a student-led environmental art exhibition aimed at raising greater awareness of and increasing commitment to sustainable practices. Through creative student artworks illustrating their unique perspectives, we hope to engage in a larger conversation on Korea’s growing environmental concerns and take responsibility for a greener future.

Chungrok: Platform

Art is a universal language, and we hope to use art as a vehicle for social change. We welcome any individuals passionate about art and sustainability. Your creativity and imagination will challenge and inspire our community to take active measures towards reducing and reversing our impact on the environment.

Join our project and help us produce the greener world we envision. Project Chungrok is proud to team up with GS Caltex, Cuchen, and BMW Handok Motors.
